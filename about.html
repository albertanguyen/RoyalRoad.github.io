How our stories begin

Once upon a time, we started on the glorious road named Royal Road - 3 spirits with the sole mission to complete: Bring the whole world - the one we used to live - the lost world back to our current dimension. Say no more! In order to complete that mission, you need a lot of time and let no one distract you from the mission. With whichever devices that you have at hand at anytime, you can take turn to play any role in the game session. Play as a team! You will never loose your chance to push your team toward the finished line.
